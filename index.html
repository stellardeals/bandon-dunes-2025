Food & Dining
3 nights · on-resort & beyond
🍺 The Gallery & Puffin Bar
Thursday, June 4 · Night 1
🐦
Post-Round · Casual
Kick off the trip at the Puffin Bar in the Main Lodge after Round 1. Italian-focused menu with panoramic views of Pacific Dunes and Bandon Dunes. Known for its relaxed vibe — perfect for the first-night recap. Dinner reservations recommended.
Italian menu Panoramic views Main Lodge Indoor + outdoor
🌊 Pacific Grill
Friday, June 5 · Night 2
🐟
Upscale · Seafood
Perched on the second level of the Pacific Dunes clubhouse, overlooking the 18th green and Punchbowl. Intimate room (~50 seats) with the best wine list on the property. Constantly rotating menu spotlighting fresh Oregon coast catches with a Mediterranean flair. Book ahead.
Seafood-forward Best wine list ~50 seats · intimate 18th green views
🥩 Ghost Tree Grill
Saturday, June 6 · Night 3
🔥
Steakhouse · Raw Bar
The marquee dinner of the trip. Pacific Northwest steakhouse and raw bar with sweeping views of Old Macdonald. Signature dry-aged 24oz ribeye from Double R Ranch, plus Kobe and Wagyu from Japan and Australia. Grand bar flows inside to an outdoor fire pit courtyard. Reviewed as "the best dinner restaurant on property, hands-down."
24oz dry-aged ribeye Wagyu & Kobe Raw bar Fire pit courtyard Old Mac views
